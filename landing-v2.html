II
§ II — The Structure

Inside the Fund.

Inside the Fund.

Portfolio

Live, per-lot.

Positions, weightings, sector exposure, risk metrics — updated each page load.

Voting

Weighted. On the record.

Every ballot carries a reason. Tied outcomes default to Hold.

Coverage

Every call, kept.

A running record of every pitch, the verdict, and the return since.

Risk

A risk panel.

Weighted beta, volatility, drawdown, concentration. Visible to portfolio managers.

Attendance

Meetings tracked.

Every pitch, ballot, and edit is logged to an audit trail.

IV
§ IV — Governance

How the Fund is run.

A hierarchy of accountability, not of privilege.

Every action that touches the portfolio is written to an audit trail that cannot be edited after the fact. Faculty advisors observe; they do not vote. The goal is not to distribute power, but responsibility.
